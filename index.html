1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Dragoster Studios ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the King vs Monsters : Platformer mobile game application ("the App") available on Google Play.

King vs Monsters : Platformer is a simple offline 2D platformer game built purely for entertainment. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever.

🛡️ Short version: We collect absolutely nothing. No data leaves your device. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. No internet required. The game works entirely on your device.

2 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

✕ Personal information ✕ Email addresses ✕ Names or usernames ✕ Device identifiers ✕ Advertising ID (GAID) ✕ Location data ✕ Usage analytics ✕ Crash reports ✕ Purchase history ✕ Contact information
3 Advertisements

King vs Monsters : Platformer contains absolutely no advertisements of any kind. There are no banner ads, video ads, interstitial ads, rewarded ads, or any other form of advertising anywhere in the App.

We do not use Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, or any other advertising SDK. We do not use the Google Advertising ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) or any equivalent identifier. This permission is not declared or used in our application.

✕ No AdMob ✕ No banner ads ✕ No video ads ✕ No interstitial ads ✕ No Advertising ID
4 Internet & Permissions

The App does not require an internet connection to function. This is a fully offline game — all gameplay happens entirely on your device with zero network communication at any time.

The App does not request any sensitive Android permissions. There is no access to your camera, microphone, contacts, storage, location, or any other device feature beyond what is required to run the game itself.

5 Login & Accounts

King vs Monsters : Platformer has no login system, no account creation, no password, and no user profile of any kind. You simply download and play — no sign-up, no registration, nothing required.

6 Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or external services of any kind. There are no connections to external servers and no data is transmitted to us or any third party at any time.

✕ No AdMob ✕ No Google Analytics ✕ No Firebase ✕ No Facebook SDK ✕ No third-party SDKs
7 Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, there is no risk to children's privacy. The App fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

8 Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by the App, there is no personal data at risk. Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only and are never sent anywhere.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Dragoster Studios ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the King vs Monsters : Platformer mobile game application ("the App") available on Google Play.

King vs Monsters : Platformer is a 2D platformer game designed for entertainment purposes. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly. However, the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain data as described below.

🛡️ Short version: We personally collect nothing. Ads are shown via Google AdMob which may collect device and usage data for ad delivery. No login required. Gameplay works offline.

2 Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or any personal information to play the game. We do not directly collect or store any personal data on our own servers.

✕ No names or emails ✕ No location data ✕ No login required ✕ No personal profiles
3 Advertisements (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

⚠️ AdMob may automatically collect and process the following information to serve and improve ads:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • IP address

This data may be used to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements, improve ad performance, and prevent fraud and abuse.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings, location, and consent. You can control ad personalization through your device settings under Google → Ads or by opting out of interest-based ads on your Android device.

For more details on how Google handles this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

✓ Google AdMob ✕ No other ad networks ✕ No direct data storage by us
4 Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection for core gameplay. Internet access is only used to load and display advertisements through Google AdMob. All gameplay mechanics function completely offline.

5 Children's Privacy

This App is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

AdMob's data collection practices for children's apps may differ. Please refer to Google's policies for more information.

6 Your Choices and Rights

You can limit or disable ad personalization through your Android device settings. Depending on your region, you may have additional rights related to your personal data under applicable privacy laws such as GDPR or CCPA.

To opt out of interest-based advertising, go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization on your Android device.

7 Data Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Third-party services such as Google AdMob handle data securely according to their own privacy policies and industry-standard security practices. We have no access to the data collected by AdMob.

8 Changes to This Policy

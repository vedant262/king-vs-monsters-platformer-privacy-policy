Privacy Policy for King vs Monsters

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

King vs Monsters is a simple 2D platformer game designed for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account creation, login, or personal sign-up to play the game.

The game itself does not directly collect personal information from users.

2. Advertisements

The game uses Google AdMob to display ads, including banner ads and rewarded/interstitial ads.

AdMob may automatically collect certain device information such as:

  • Device type
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App interaction data

This data is handled by Google AdMob according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for children and general audiences. We aim to comply with Google Play Families policies where applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdMob

5. Data Security

We do not store personal user data on our own servers.
